Life in Chicago

The weather in Chicago is very unpredictable. The running joke is that you can experience all four seasons in one day! The seasons are:
  • Winter
  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Fall

The top reasons I love living in Chicago are:

  1. There is a lot to do.
  2. The people are (generally) pretty friendly.
  3. The food is excellent.
  4. It is easy to get to different parts of the country because it is centrally located.
